A Tribute to India's Culinary Soul

Food Garden was born from a single idea: that the best Indian food is inseparable from the land it comes from — its soil, its seasons, and its stories passed down through generations.

From the ghee-laden kitchens of Punjab to the coconut forests of Kerala, we bring the full spectrum of Indian regional cooking to your plate, enriched by the freshness of our own organic garden.

Our Journey

From a humble kitchen dream to a beloved Bengaluru institution.

The Beginning

Chef Arjun Nair opens Food Garden as a 20-seat café in Ashok Nagar with 12 dishes and a dream.

2010

First Award

Recognized by Bengaluru Times as one of the "Top 10 Authentic Indian Restaurants" in the city.

2013

The Garden Expansion

We open an organic garden adjacent to the restaurant, supplying 60% of all herbs and vegetables.

2016

50,000 Guests Milestone

We celebrate serving our 50,000th guest and expand to a 120-seat garden restaurant.

2024

What We Stand For

Three pillars that guide every dish we serve.

Authentic Recipes

Sourced from regional Indian kitchens — from the ghee-laden gravies of Punjab to the coconut-kissed curries of Kerala.

Fresh & Seasonal

Our in-house garden supplies herbs, greens & edible flowers year-round. We change our menu with the seasons.

Cooked with Love

Every plate carries the care and pride of our chefs, who treat cooking as a craft, not a production line.
